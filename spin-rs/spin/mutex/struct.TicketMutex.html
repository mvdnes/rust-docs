A ticket lock for mutual exclusion based on spinning.

A ticket lock is similair to a queue management system. When a thread tries to lock the values, it is assigned a ticket, and it spins until its ticket is the next in line. When releasing the value, the next ticket will be processed.

pub unsafe fn force_unlock(&self)[src]

Force unlock the ticket lock, by serving the next ticket.

Safety

This is extremely unsafe if the lock is not held by the current thread. However, this can be useful in some instances for exposing the lock to FFI that doesn't know how to deal with RAII.
